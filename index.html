Tutorial

How to Play:

1) Click a square. Any square.

2) A square can change any of it's neighbor's color too.

3) Get all squares to final color to win.

4) Click refresh if you get stuck.

Features

-Change colors and sound effects.

-100 basic levels.

-25 unlockable levels.

-Wait, where's R?
